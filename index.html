About me

I am a Research Scientist and Invited Assistant Professor in the ESRGv3 lab at University of Minho, Portugal. My research focus on virtualization for mixed-critical systems, with special attention on minimizing interference for predictability and real-time guarantees. I also focus on trusted execution environments for both high-end (mobile) and low-end (IoT) systems.
