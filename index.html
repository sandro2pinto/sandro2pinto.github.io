About me

I am a Research Scientist and Invited Assistant Professor in the ESRGv3 lab at University of Minho, Portugal. I work in the intersection of operating systems and virtualization, real-time, security, and computer architectures. I aim at designing and building embedded, cyber-physical, and Internet of Things (IoT) systems that make our transports, industries, and core infrastructures more efficient, predictable, reliable, safe, and secure. Over the last few years, I have been mainly focused on understanding how to leverage Arm TrustZone technology to provide an alternative form of system virtualization. Today, my group and I are especially focused on developing trustworthy hypervisors and trusted execution environments (TEE) for modern (low- and high-end) heterogeneous commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) hardware.
